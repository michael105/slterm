Colors

Colors can be printed at the shell with 'echo -e \"\e[A;Nm\"'

A: one of 0..7, text attributes
N: fg color 30 - 37, and bg color 40 - 47

255 colors are available with: 'echo -e \"\e[A;38;5;Nm\"',
and 'echo -e \"\e[0,48,5,Nm'\" for the background.
A: 0=normal, 1=light, 2=dark
