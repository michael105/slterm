


Some of the ansi escape sequences,
also documented in ./doc/..




'\e[X1m'
'\e[X1;X2m'
'\e[X1;X2;X3m'
...


X1..X defines color and attributes.


30..37: foreground color
40..47: background color

colors, in order:

black 
red 
green 
yellow(brown) 
blue 
magenta 
cyan 
white(gray) 


Attribute:
0 	clear all attributes
1	bold
2	faint
3	cursive
4	underline
5	blink
6	blink
7	inverse


attributes can be combined, also 1 and 2



255 colors:

foreground: '\e[38;5;XXm'

xx one of 16-255

background: '\e[48;5;XXm'


cursor:

'\e[X q'

X:
1,2: block cursor
3,4: underline
5,6: vertical bar
  7: 'X'
